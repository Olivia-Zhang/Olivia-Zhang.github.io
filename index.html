Brief Biography

I am a 5th year Ph.D. candidate in Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Minyi Guo.
and Prof. Quan Chen.
. I am interested in cloud computing, microservices, GPU resource management, ML for systems and computer architecture. My research focuses on QoS-aware resource management in co-located datacenters and managing GPU microservices in datacenters, improving their performance and resource efficiency. Before studying at SJTU, I obtained my B.Eng. degree in Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) in 2017. I was a Ph.D. research intern at Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA) in 2020 and 2021, hosted by Zhenhua Han. During my internship, I did research on improving resource efficiency of cloud gaming datacenters.
